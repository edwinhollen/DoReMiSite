DoReMi

A music puzzle game

What is DoReMi?

DoReMi is a music-based puzzle game, designed to encourage music education through listening to music, and identifying the notes.

Where is DoReMi?

DoReMi is currently in development for Windows, OS X, Linux, as well as Android and iOS. See the current development status on GitHub.

The developer

DoReMi is being developed and maintained by Edwin Hollen, student at West Virginia Wesleyan College, as a senior project. Check me out on GitHub, or LinkedIn.
